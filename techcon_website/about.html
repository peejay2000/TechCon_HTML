Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local meetup for developers and technology enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into an international event that brings together innovators, educators, and entrepreneurs from around the globe.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and empower individuals through technology education and networking opportunities. We aim to make cutting-edge tech accessible to all, regardless of background or skill level.
